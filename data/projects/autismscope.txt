Project Name

AutismScope – Machine Learning Powered Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) Prediction System

Project Type

Machine Learning | Healthcare AI | Full Stack Web Application | Predictive Analytics

Overview

AutismScope is an AI-powered web application designed to assist in the early screening of Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) using machine learning. The platform enables users to complete a structured assessment questionnaire and receive an instant prediction indicating the likelihood of ASD.

The application combines a modern React frontend with a Flask backend and a trained machine learning model to provide a fast, user-friendly, and accessible screening experience.

AutismScope is intended as an educational and research-based decision support tool and is not a replacement for professional medical diagnosis.

--------------------------------------------------------------------

Problem Statement

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental condition that affects communication, social interaction, and behavior.

Early identification of ASD is extremely important because early intervention can significantly improve learning, communication, and developmental outcomes.

However, traditional diagnosis often involves:

• Multiple clinical evaluations
• Long waiting periods
• Limited access to specialists
• High diagnostic costs
• Geographic barriers in rural or underserved areas

Many families are unable to obtain timely assessments, delaying treatment and support.

AutismScope aims to provide a quick, accessible, and data-driven preliminary screening tool that can assist individuals and healthcare professionals by identifying potential ASD indicators early.
